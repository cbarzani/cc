# CC Film Website Design Guidelines

## Aesthetic Stance
**Cinematic Sophistication** — A dark, high-contrast design inspired by cinema and film premieres. Rich blacks, dramatic lighting effects, warm gold accents, and serif typography that recalls classic Hollywood elegance combined with modern minimalism.

## Typography
- **Display/Headings**: Playfair Display — elegant serif with dramatic contrast, perfect for film titles and hero headings
- **Body Text**: Inter — clean, highly readable sans-serif for content and navigation
- **Letter Spacing**: Add generous tracking on hero headings for dramatic effect

## Color Palette
- **Background**: Deep cinematic black (#0a0a0a) with subtle gradient overlays
- **Text**: Warm off-white (#f5f5f0) for high contrast readability
- **Accent**: Rich gold (#d4af37) for calls-to-action and highlights
- **Secondary**: Warm gray (#9ca3af) for supporting text
- **Card backgrounds**: Dark charcoal (#1a1a1a) with subtle transparency

## Layout Philosophy
- **Hero-driven**: Large, cinematic hero sections with dramatic typography
- **Grid-based portfolio**: Showcase film work in elegant grid layouts
- **Asymmetric compositions**: Break from standard centered layouts where appropriate
- **Generous whitespace**: Let content breathe, even against dark backgrounds
- **Layered depth**: Use subtle shadows and overlays to create dimension

## Visual Treatment
- Use gradient overlays on images to maintain text legibility
- Implement smooth transitions and hover states (0.3s ease)
- Add subtle shadows for depth
- Include cinematic 16:9 aspect ratios for video/image showcases
- Employ generous spacing using rem units

## Sections
1. **Hero**: Bold statement about CC Film's vision with dramatic typography
2. **About**: Company mission and creative approach
3. **Portfolio/Showcase**: Grid of film work with hover effects
4. **Services**: What CC Film offers
5. **Distribution Partnership**: Call for distributors with contact info
6. **Contact/Footer**: Clear contact information and social links

## Interactive Elements
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Hover effects that reveal additional information
- Subtle scale transforms on hover (scale: 1.02)
- Video preview capabilities
- Working navigation with smooth scrolling
